TRANSPARENT PRICING

KEY PRICING FACTORS

Distance & Route

The total transport cost is primarily calculated based on mileage and route efficiency. Longer distances naturally increase the cost due to fuel, labor, and time. Furthermore, the route plays a vital role: transport on high-volume, major interstate corridors (like I-95 or I-80) is typically more affordable and faster than servicing remote, low-volume rural towns, which require special detours and extended scheduling.

Season & Urgency

Price and schedule are heavily influenced by the time of year and required speed. Demand and rates increase during peak seasons (summer, holidays), limiting carrier availability. Urgent Shipping prioritizes your vehicle for the fastest route but incurs a premium fee. For the most favorable rates, we recommend booking during off-peak seasons with flexible dates.

Vehicle Value & Type

Moving high-value automobiles—such as luxury brands, supercars, or irreplaceable classics—involves enhanced insurance policies and requires the use of specialized enclosed equipment for maximum protection against road debris and weather. This necessary level of specialized care and increased liability coverage makes the transport of these particular vehicle types significantly more expensive than standard shipments.

Vehicle Condition & Loading

The operational status of your vehicle directly affects the transport cost. Vehicles that are **inoperable** (cannot roll, steer, or brake) require specialized equipment like winches and increased labor time for safe loading and securing. This additional complexity and effort for handling non-running vehicles results in a mandatory **inoperable fee** added to the standard shipping rate.

COMMON CONCERNS

FAQS

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes, every vehicle transported under our service is fully insured from the moment of pickup until safe delivery. Details regarding coverage limits are provided in your personalized quote.

How long does the transport process take?

Transit times vary based on distance and route. Cross-country shipments typically take 7–14 days. We offer expedited services for tighter deadlines.

Do you offer open or enclosed transport?

We specialize in enclosed transport for maximum protection of luxury, classic, and exotic vehicles. Open transport is available upon client request.

Can I ship personal items in the vehicle?

For safety and insurance reasons, we strongly recommend against shipping personal items. If necessary, a maximum of 100 lbs of soft goods may be permitted, but these items are not covered by our transport insurance.

The founder of JP Auto Transport was born in Romania, a land of resilience, tradition, and passion. At only seven years old, he moved to the United States, but those Romanian roots have always remained alive within him — strong, unshaken, and enduring, like the branches of an ancient oak. Growing up in America taught him discipline, innovation, and precision in logistics. Yet, at every step of his journey, he kept the determination and heart of his Romanian heritage. This unique blend — the strength inherited from Romania and the opportunities and professionalism of the U.S. — gave birth to a company that does more than move cars.
